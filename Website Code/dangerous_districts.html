Most Dangerous Districts Analysis using Tableau

Insights form Most Dangerous Districts Analysis using Tableau

The first dashboard, Most Dangerous Districts, provides an interactive way to explore the criminal incidents that have occurred throughout Washington, D.C. The above map is divided by each of the 7 districts of the city using different colors. Thereâ€™s a filter on the right where a user can select different years to see how the density of the incidents per district changes. As the incidents change on the map, a further breakdown below can be referred to as well.

The stacked bar chart on the left provides an overall reference while switching through the different years. The table on the right breaks down the number of incidents by time of the day. For example and by default, 2019 is selected and the District 2 of the city appears to have the highest criminal incidents for the year while occurring during the evening shift. Perhaps local law enforcement show explore the map in District 2 to identify possible patterns of the incidents occurring during the evening hours.
